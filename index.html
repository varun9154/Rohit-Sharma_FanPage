About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and captain of the Indian national cricket team. Known for his exceptional talent, he holds the record for the highest individual score in a one-day international match. His journey from the streets of Mumbai to leading India to victories is nothing short of extraordinary.
