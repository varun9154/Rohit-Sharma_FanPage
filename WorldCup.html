The Journey to Glory

Leading Team India to Victory

As captain, Rohit Sharma led India with resilience and strategy in the 2024 ICC T20 World Cup. His calm demeanor under pressure and sharp decision-making helped guide the team to victory.

Record-Breaking Century

Rohit played a sensational knock, scoring a century in the semi-finals, breaking records and leading the team to the finals. His innings displayed sheer class and an unmatched understanding of the game.

The Historic Win

Under Rohit's captaincy, India clinched their second T20 World Cup title. The victory in the final was a testament to teamwork, skill, and the leadership of Rohit Sharma, making the nation proud once again.
