Milestone Moments

264 Runs in an ODI

The highest individual score in ODI cricket, Rohit's incredible 264 against Sri Lanka in 2014 remains one of the most iconic innings ever.

Three Double Centuries in ODIs

No one else has managed even two, but Rohit has shattered records with his four double centuries in ODIs.

100+ Sixes in T20 Internationals

Rohit Sharma was the first Indian player to reach 100 sixes in T20 Internationals, showcasing his explosive power.

Historic Wins & Leadership

5 IPL Titles as Captain

Leading Mumbai Indians, Rohit Sharma has become one of the most successful captains in IPL history, winning 5 titles.

Five Centuries in 2019 World Cup

Rohit set a World Cup record by smashing five centuries in a single edition, reaffirming his place among the legends.

200+ T20 International Matches

Rohit Sharma was the first cricketer to play over 200 T20 Internationals, marking his dominance in the shortest format.
